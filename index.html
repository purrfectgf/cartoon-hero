Sasuke Uchiha

About Sasuke

Sasuke is one of the main characters in the anime "Naruto". Sasuke is much more than your archetype of a loner. He's not only a member of the acclaimed Uchiha clan of Konoha village, he's also considered a prodigy in his ninjutsu skills. He's determined to keep training to fulfill his revenge against the one who murdered his clan. Additionally, he trains alongside his best friend and rival Naruto, a ninja with a big smile and a surprising amount of strength.

Sasuke and Team 7

Sasuke's Characteristics

  1. He has Sharingan eyes, a special ability only given to those within the Uchiha clan, which allows them to see others' chakra.
  2. Sasuke often holds himself to a high standard to the point where he sometimes to refuse to interact with those beneath him, but has a soft spot for those who he cares about.
  3. Sasuke tends to often wear darker colors such as black, gray or so on, in contrast to his rival Naruto who wears his signature orange or brighter colors.

Sasuke's Team

Before meeting Team 7, Sasuke was determined to learn and train on his own. He ends up becoming part of Team 7 in the beginning of Naruto, despite not wanting to be part of a team. Initially, his only goal was to train for vengeance. However, over time, he grows to admire and care for the people on his team such as his teacher Kakashi, and the other two members of the team, Naruto and Sakura, to the point where he would even risk his life for them.
